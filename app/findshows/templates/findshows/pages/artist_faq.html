Artist FAQ

Posting shows

{% accordion_element title="How do I create an artist profile/get access to post shows?" %} There are two ways to get artist access! If you know somebody who already has artist access, they can invite you via their Settings page (or via the concert creation page if you have a show coming up together). If not, go to your Settings page and click Create Artist Profile. A mod will review your profile for verification as soon as possible. {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="How do I post a show?" %} Log in; from the menu, go to your artist dashboard and click Create new show listing. Fill out all of the information about the show. Content from your artist profile and the profiles of other artists on the bill will be automatically populated in the concert listing. {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="How do I edit a show I'm a part of?" %} Only the user who initially created a show can edit it. Log in; from the menu, go to your artist dashboard. If you created a show, it should have an Edit button next to it. If you just want to edit your artist info it appears on the concert listing, you can do that by editing your artist profile (also from the dashboard). {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="How do I delete/cancel a show I'm a part of?" %} Only the user who initially created a show can cancel it. Log in; from the menu, go to your artist dashboard. If you created a show, it should have a Cancel button next to it. The concert's detail page will now display prominently the fact that it is cancelled, and it will no longer show up anywhere else (search, artist pages, emails). You cannot delete a show. {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="How do I give a bandmate/manager access to list shows?" %} Multiple users can manage the same artist. Log in; from the menu, go to your artist dashboard. Click Manage access under the relevant artist, enter the email of the person to whom you'd like togive access, click Add, then Submit. {% endaccordion_element %}

Followers

{% accordion_element title="What do followers see?" %} Followers receive one weekly email for all the bands they follow. Your shows will be included (a) the week they are announced and (b) the week before they happen. {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="How do I get more followers?" %} You can find a link and a QR code for each artist on your artist dashboard. Share the link to your existing socials to convert followers, and print out the QR code for your merch table. There are also discovery tools for listeners on the site that can lead to new followers, but we recommend an active approach! {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="Who can see who follows me?" %} Nobody! Including you! You can see a follower count for each artist on your artist dashboard, but that is not made public. {% endaccordion_element %} {% accordion_element title="Can I share other news (e.g. releases) with my followers?" %} Soon! We're working on deciding the best way to implement this without abusing the attention of listeners. Potential designs include (a) being able to announce releases in the same way you can currently announce shows (i.e. a form with all the relevant images and links) or (b) being able to syndicate your blog (e.g. Substack) or Bluesky to the weekly email. Feel free to be in touch with ideas & feedback! {% endaccordion_element %}
